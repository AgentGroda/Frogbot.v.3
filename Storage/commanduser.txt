**Commands**
`addmessages` **Adds messages to your message count. Only for Bot Owner!**
`addmoney` **Adds money to your balance. Only for Bot Owner!**
`ascii` **Ascii some text.**
`balance` **Check your balance.**
`botinfo` **Get info about the bot.**
`bug` **Report a bug.**
`channeldelete` **Deletes the channel you are in. Needs Administration perms!**
`createchannel` **Creates a channel. Needs Administration perms!**
`createinvite` **Creates an invite to your channel.**
`creatensfwchannel` **Creates a nsfw channel. Needs Administration perms!**
`day` **Check the current time in your timezone.**
`discrim` **Check how many people who has your and others discrim.**
`dog` **Get a random dog.**
`embed` **Creates an Embed.**
`emojis` **Shows all custom emojis on your server.**
`eval` **Eval code. Only for Bot Owner!**
`guilds` **Shows a list of all guilds.**
`help` **Sends this list.**
`invite` **Get a invite link to the bot.**
`invites` **Get a list of all invites.**
